ABOUT

Animefest, a time and place where animanga lovers, artists, and entertainers celebrate what we love! On May 7, 2022, participants go to the Price Center at UCSD to attend discussion and workshop panels, buy the best artwork, and check out your favorite manga in our manga library! And of course, there will be cosplays, festival games, and video gamesâ€”all for some otaku fun.

Main Stage and Raffles

Our annual Cosplay Masquerade and raffles are held on the Main Stage in West Ballroom.

Exhibitor Hall

Browse fanmade merchandise drawn or designed by local artists in the Artist Alley, or check out items sold by industry vendors.

Theater

Performances and movie showings will be held in the Price Center Theater.

Panels

We have informational and interactive panels for your enjoyment run by students, alumni, and professionals. Check them out!

Manga Library

Relax and check out a book from Anime & Manga Enthusiasts's library of donated manga.

Gaming

We have a Super Smash Bros. Ultimate tournament with prizes, brought to you by Triton Smash!

Featured Activities

Have fun with the Board Game and Origami Folders clubs, and try out the Cardfight!! Vanguard card game.

MAP AND SCHEDULE

Animefest is held at the Price Center at UC San Diego. Specifically, it will be at the West Ballroom, Thurgood Marshall, Eleanor Roosevelt, Roger Revelle, Theater, Red Shoe, Bear, and Green Table rooms.

All rooms are located on the second floor with the exception of the Theater, which is on the first floor and below the Bear Room.

PARKING

Unfortunately, parking is not free on UCSD's campus. We recommend you find free parking off campus and walking to campus or taking the bus to campus. We apologize for the inconvenience.

For parking on campus, please take a look at the official campus map, expand Parking by Permit Type, and select Visitor in the menu to see areas where you can park. Please ensure that you park in designated Visitor spots which will be marked on the floor in these areas.
